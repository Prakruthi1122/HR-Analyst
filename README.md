# HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview

This HR Analytics project aims to analyze employee data and uncover insights related to workforce demographics, employee attrition, job satisfaction, compensation, performance, and work-life balance. The project leverages SQL, Excel, Power BI, Tableau, and PowerPoint to transform raw HR data into actionable business insights.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Employee attrition and workforce management are critical challenges for organizations. This project helps identify patterns in employee turnover, understand workforce demographics, and support data-driven HR decision-making.

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains **1,473 employee records** and **39 attributes**, including:

- Employee ID
- Age & Age Group
- Gender
- Department
- Job Role
- Education Field
- Monthly Income
- Attrition Status
- Job Satisfaction
- Environment Satisfaction
- Performance Rating
- Work-Life Balance
- Overtime
- Total Working Years
- Years at Company
- Years Since Last Promotion
